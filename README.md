# UK SME Financial Health Dashboard

## Overview
Analysed 16 years of ONS Annual Business Survey data (2008–2023) 
across UK Retail, Hospitality and Construction sectors to identify 
financial stress indicators and deteriorating trends.

## Tools
- Microsoft Excel (data cleaning, ratio calculation, stress scoring)
- Looker Studio (4-page interactive dashboard)

## Data Source
ONS Annual Business Survey — the same source used by HM Treasury 
for UK sector monitoring

## Key Findings
1. Retail classified as persistently High stress — scoring maximum 
   stress flags every year from 2019–2023
2. Retail shows dual deterioration in 2023 — gross margin fell 0.82 
   percentage points while cost ratio simultaneously increased to 0.788
3. Hospitality shows deteriorating trend in 2023 despite post-Covid 
   recovery — gross margin fell 1.1 percentage points
4. Construction is the most resilient sector — consistent improvement 
   across all indicators since 2020

## Stress Scoring Methodology
- High stress: Cost Ratio > 0.75 OR Gross Margin < 25%
- Moderate: Cost Ratio 0.60–0.75
- Low: Cost Ratio < 0.60
- Thresholds defined prior to data exploration
